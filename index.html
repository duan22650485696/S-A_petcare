House-sitting / Snowbird Service

This service gives you peace of mind when you are travelling - short-or long-term - knowing your home is being cared for while you are away. Available services include mail retrieval, lights on/off, plant-watering, small animal care, grass-cutting and shoveling. We can report to you as requested. Other services may be available, upon request.

Pet-sitting Services

This service is an alternative to boarding or kenneling your pets. Pets can be cared for in your home or ours. Services in your home can include feeding/watering, litter box cleaning, walking, and social time. Services in our home include feeding, exercise, and snuggles and social time. (We have a large, fenced yard for plenty of opportunity for exercise.)

Other Services

We can be at your home to let contractors, service people, real estate agents, etc., inside, as requested.
